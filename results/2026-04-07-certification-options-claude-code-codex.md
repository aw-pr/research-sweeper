## Summary

Between January 2026 and April 2026, Anthropic launched a substantive technical certification programme anchored in its Claude Partner Network. OpenAI launched broadly targeted AI literacy certifications, but has not released a Codex-specific or developer-level certification programme to date.

---

## Anthropic

### Claude Certified Architect (CCA) — Foundations

Launched: **12 March 2026** as part of the Claude Partner Network announcement.

**What it is:** The first official Anthropic technical certification. A proctored, closed-book, 60-question exam (120 minutes, no AI assistance permitted) that tests the ability to design and ship production-grade Claude applications at enterprise scale.

**Topics covered:**
- Agentic architecture design
- MCP (Model Context Protocol) integration
- Claude Code workflows and CI/CD pipelines
- Prompt engineering and context management
- Multi-agent system design and failure handling

**Prerequisites:** At least 6 months of hands-on production experience with the Claude API, Claude Code, and MCP. This is explicitly a practitioner credential, not an AI literacy badge.

**Cost:** Free for the first 5,000 employees of organisations in the Claude Partner Network; $99 per attempt thereafter.

**Source quality note:** The official announcement at [anthropic.com/news/claude-partner-network](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-partner-network) is the authoritative source. Secondary community coverage (Medium, DEV.to, lowcode.agency) is consistent with that primary source.

### Anthropic Academy

Launched: **2 March 2026** — ten days before the CCA exam went live.

13 free, self-paced courses hosted on Skilljar (no subscription or paywall). These are the official preparation path for the CCA. A "Claude Code in Action" course is also available on Coursera.

**Citations:**
- [Anthropic Courses — Skilljar](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/)
- [Claude Code in Action — Anthropic Courses](https://anthropic.skilljar.com/claude-code-in-action)
- [Claude Code in Action — Coursera](https://www.coursera.org/learn/claude-code-in-action)

### Claude Partner Network

Announced: **12 March 2026** with a committed $100 million investment for 2026.

Membership is free. Partners gain access to training materials, co-marketing support, dedicated Applied AI engineers for live deals, and the certification programme. Anchor partners include Accenture, Deloitte, Cognizant, and Infosys.

### Roadmap (later 2026)

Anthropic has confirmed additional certifications in development:
- Seller certification
- Developer certification
- Advanced Architect certification

No dates announced as of 2026-04-07.

**Citation:** [Anthropic invests $100M — The Next Web](https://thenextweb.com/news/anthropic-commits-100m-to-claude-partner-network)

## OpenAI

### AI Foundations Certification

OpenAI launched its first certification courses via [OpenAI Academy](https://academy.openai.com/) and Coursera. The AI Foundations course is embedded directly in ChatGPT (learners complete real tasks, receive in-context feedback, and earn a course certificate). Badges are issued via **Credly**; psychometric standards are validated by **ETS**.

**Target audience:** General workforce and enterprise employees — not a developer or API-level credential. Pilot employer partners include Walmart, Lowe's, Boston Consulting Group, Accenture, Upwork, and public-sector organisations.

**Citation:** [Launching our first OpenAI Certifications courses — OpenAI](https://openai.com/index/openai-certificate-courses/)

### ChatGPT Foundations for Teachers

Available on Coursera. Covers ChatGPT essentials for classroom and administrative use.

### Codex-Specific Certification

**None found.** As of 2026-04-07, OpenAI has not launched a Codex-specific certification or a developer/API-level credential. The Codex agent is now available inside GitHub Copilot for Business and Pro users (since February 2026), but no associated certification programme exists.

**Citation:** [Claude and Codex now available for Copilot Business & Pro users — GitHub Changelog](https://github.blog/changelog/2026-02-26-claude-and-codex-now-available-for-copilot-business-pro-users/)

OpenAI's stated goal is to certify **10 million Americans by 2030** through the AI Foundations track.

## Key Takeaways

1. **Anthropic has the only production-grade technical certification** for AI coding tools launched in this window. The CCA Foundations is meaningfully differentiated from AI literacy programmes — it requires real API and Claude Code experience.
2. **OpenAI's certifications are broad workforce enablement** tools, not developer credentials. No Codex-specific or API-level certification exists as of April 2026.
3. **Anthropic Academy (free, 13 courses)** is the recommended preparation path for the CCA; available immediately with no paywall.
4. **More credentials are coming from both sides** later in 2026 — Anthropic's developer and advanced architect tiers will be the ones to watch for practitioners.
